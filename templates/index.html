Our Story

Katie and Carlos first met in the summer of 2012 when they ended up subletting the same apartment in Ithaca, NY for summer classes. Katie was relieved when Carlos short term sublet was up and he moved out, leaving her to have the apartment for herself for the remainder of the summer. However, weeks before their fall co-op internship in Palo Alto, CA began they were notified that they would be sharing an apartment again for the duration of the fall internship.

They started dating, even as Katie spent the following spring semester studying abroad in Barcelona. Throughout the next summer back in Palo Alto together, and the following two years at Cornell, their relationship flourished.

After Cornell, Katie and Carlos spent a year in a long-distance relationship, with Katie in Boston, MA and Carlos in Charlotte, NC. Katie then moved to Charlotte, where they spent the next 4 years developing their careers and more importantly, their partnership together.

On May 30, 2021, We Said Yes!

The couple now live in Chicago (at least until the summer of 2022) and are excited for life's next chapters!

Getting to Cartagena

Airport

We recommend arriving by plane to Cartagena's Rafael Núñez International Airport (CTG), which is located about a 15 minute taxi ride from all of the recommended hotels. From the U.S., there are non-stop flights from Miami (MIA), Fort Lauderdale (FLL), and New York (JFK). Layovers in Miami (MIA), Bogotá (BOG), Medellín (MDE), or Panamá (PTY) are common for one-stop itineraries from the United States or Europe.

Airlines

In addition to well-known U.S. and European airlines, other reliable companies serving regular commercial flights to the Cartagena airport (CTG) include:

  • Avianca (Colombia's flag carrier)
  • Copa Airlines (Panama's flag carrier)
  • LATAM Airlines

Low-cost carriers Spirit Airlines and Viva Air also fly into Cartagena. However, due to reliability issues, we do not recommend booking through these airlines if other alternatives exist.
